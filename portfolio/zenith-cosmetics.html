Zenith Cosmetics

A minimalist, single-page site for a fictional beauty and cosmetics salon



The central theme of this project was minimalism. My aim was to create a sleek and easy to navigate site that would be useful to hypothetical clients while not being overbearing on the amount of information

The hero image which first greets the visitor was perfect for this project, because it is relevant to both the type of company and the minimal design aspect. I chose a colour palette of light pink and mintcream to contrast with dark text, and kept this consistent throughout.

Due to its unconventional layout, with text alternating its position from one section to the next, the responsive element of this site was a bit trickier than usual. I ran with the idea of full page background images with text overlayed as I found this to be aesthetically pleasing and gave me the chance to experiment with different layouts and positioning.



Note that this is a site for a fictional client; external links and forms are inactive
