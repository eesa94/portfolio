Save Our Date

A simple wedding announcement



The aim behind this project was to provide a simple, single-page wedding announcement which could be sent to friends and family as a link via messaging, email, and social media.

At the initial announcement stage, the intention was to provide the minimal amount of information to potential guests so that they could plan well in advance.

At a later date, the back end will be built with Rails, allowing the implementation of an RSVP mechanism, where guests will accept or decline their invitation. Specific wedding details are private, so guests will be required to log in with a provided password, and the guestlist will be tracked and shared with the client.
