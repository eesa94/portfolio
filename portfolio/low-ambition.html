Low Ambition

A single-page informational website for a car enthusiast collective



The central idea around this site was keeping it simple as it would be used to provide basic information about the client to prospective users.

Nowadays, most interaction would take place via social media, so I decided to keep the level of detail to a minimum, as it is likely that visitors would already have a good idea of what the client does, so it would act primarily as a 'formal' online presence to complement their social media presence.

I used a single background image for the entire site, to maintain continuity and in keeping with the theme of simplicity. This also inspired the simple colour scheme of dark blue and faded yellow. 

Developing a single-page site was in keeping with the idea that mobile users would make up the majority of traffic, so minimising complications in the user flow was key.



Note that this is a site for a fictional client; external links and forms are inactive
